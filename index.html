What We Do

We collaborate with our partner organizations, institutions and industry professionals to provide services
essential to optimizing research quality and impact for professionals and enthusiasts
1
Training
We offer trainings and certifications
on contemporary research
methods and practices
2
Publishing
We offer high quality open-access peer-reviewed research publishing
along with digitization, branding and digital marketing services for researchers
3
Consulting
We carry out as NEEDS, assessments, MEL, and other research projects on behalf of companies, governments and
local/international NGOs
4
Membership
We induct and maintain a database of highly competent researchers as members of the OASIS Research Community

Membership Benefits

We operate membership system that delivers valuable benefits for members including subsidized
access to our resources, networking, sharing of information on valuable opportunities, and paid consultancies, among other benefits.
Information
Frequent updates on research opportunities in academics and other industries, e.g. grants
Payment
Paid participation on research consulting projects for our corporate clients and public sector.
Services
Up to 100% waivers on publishing and author visibility services for subscribed members.

About Us

OASIS Research Community was established to advance the practice of high-quality research by providing research training and support to research enthusiasts and professionals around the world, particularly from developing countries like Nigeria. OASIS creates and operates platforms that promote and optimize intellectual insights, research capabilities and research outcomes for our members and corporate clients.
Join Us

Our Vision

To sustainably optimize academic and professional research outcomes for stakeholders

Our Mission

To entrench global research best practices in the academic and professional research community
through research education, support, publishing, and marketing

Our Core Values

Excellence. Ethics. Integrity.

Our Recognition
